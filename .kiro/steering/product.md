# Product Overview

AustamGood WMS is a warehouse management system designed for medium to large-scale businesses in Thailand. The system provides comprehensive functionality for managing warehouse operations including:

- **Inbound Operations**: Receiving goods from suppliers with barcode/QR scanning, pallet management, and quality control
- **Warehouse Management**: Location management, inventory tracking, and stock movements
- **Outbound Operations**: Order fulfillment, picking, packing, and shipping
- **Production**: Production order management and material issue tracking
- **Master Data**: Management of SKUs, customers, suppliers, employees, warehouses, and locations
- **Mobile Support**: PWA-enabled mobile interface for warehouse floor operations
- **Reporting**: Dashboard analytics and operational reports

The system is bilingual (Thai/English) with Thai as the primary language for the UI. It emphasizes barcode scanning workflows, real-time inventory tracking, and mobile-first design for warehouse workers.
